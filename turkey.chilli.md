## 4 Bean Turkey Chilli  

`2` Red onion     
`2 LB` ground Turkey

`3 T` Oil        
`2 T` Chicken Masala    
`1 T` Chili Powder    
`1 t` Haldi    
`2 t` Garam Masala    
`1 t` Cumin    

* In a skillet caramalize onion, add spices. 
* Add turkey and brown it.   
* In a crockpot, combine the above with the ingredients below.
